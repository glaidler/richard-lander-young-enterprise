The Journey So Far

1977 - Mousehole, Cornwall

Born at Sea

Hand-knitted by Martha Pengelly for her husband Tom, a mackerel fisherman. The distinctive cable pattern was her own design - meant to bring him luck on rough seas.

1995 - St Ives

The Art Student Years

Found in a charity shop by Jamie, an art student who wore it to every gallery opening. Picked up some paint splatters (now a feature, not a flaw!) and a coffee stain that tells its own story.

2019 - Bristol

Vintage Shop Discovery

Sat on a rail at "Second Time Around" vintage shop for two years, waiting for its next adventure. Acquired that authentic vintage shop smell (lavender and possibility).

2026 - Loop HQ

The Glow-Up

Rescued by the Loop crew! We've repaired the elbow patches, added custom embroidered waves, and given it a proper spa day. This legend is ready for chapter four.
