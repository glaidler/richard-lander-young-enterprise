The Journey So Far

1989 - Los Angeles

The Birth of Cool

Bought from a Levi's store on Melrose Avenue by Cindy, who was definitely going to start a band. She added the first patch - a Guns N' Roses logo that's still there, faded but defiant.

1996 - Seattle

The Grunge Chapter

Picked up at a garage sale by Marcus, a coffee shop barista with excellent taste. Added a Nirvana patch and wore it to three actual concerts. Kurt would've approved.

2005 - Berlin

The European Adventure

Travelled to Germany in a backpacker's rucksack. Spent five years in Berlin's club scene, collecting vintage pins and good vibes. Added a peace sign patch during a festival.

2015 - Brighton

The Seaside Years

Found at a car boot sale by Priya, who wore it to every indie gig on the south coast. Added a "Save The Planet" patch (how fitting for its future with Loop!).

2026 - Loop HQ

The Ultimate Remix

This jacket's been through three decades of music history. We've reinforced the seams, fixed a torn pocket, and added our own Loop patch. The collection of patches stays - each one's a passport stamp from its life.
