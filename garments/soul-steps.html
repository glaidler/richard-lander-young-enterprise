The Journey So Far

2016 - New York City

Fresh Out the Box

Bought on a gap year trip to NYC by Alex, who was "finding themselves." First customisation: Sharpie doodles on the rubber sole (still visible if you look closely). Walked across Brooklyn Bridge on day one.

2017-2020 - University of Leeds

The Student Years

These Chucks did the rounds. Library all-nighters. Student union floors. That one time they got lost at a house party and came back with mystery stickers. Signed on the side by flatmates at graduation.

2021 - Camden Market, London

Market Days

Sold to Riley, a market trader who wore them every weekend rain or shine. Picked up authentic market patina: coffee splashes, vintage record dust, and good karma.

2026 - Loop HQ

The Sole Resurrection

We've deep cleaned these legends, refreshed the laces with recycled cotton ones, restored the rubber, and kept every signature and doodle intact. These shoes have walked through stories - now they're ready for yours.
