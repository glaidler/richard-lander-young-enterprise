The Journey So Far

2012 - Milan Fashion Week

The Runway Debut

Created for an Italian designer's spring collection. These jeans walked the runway exactly once, worn by a model named Luca who said they were "the comfiest thing he'd ever worn backstage."

2013 - Shoreditch, London

The Influencer Era

Bought at a sample sale by lifestyle blogger @vintageviv. Featured in 47 Instagram posts, one TikTok that got 2M views, and spilled matcha on the left knee (now a vibe).

2018 - Manchester

The Band Years

Donated to a charity shop where Maz, lead guitarist of "The Static," snapped them up. Survived two UK tours, countless rehearsals, and a backstage incident involving a fog machine.

2022 - Depop

The Digital Marketplace

Listed as "vintage designer, slight wear" - sat in someone's wardrobe for two years unworn. The fashion industry's loss is Loop's gain.

2026 - Loop HQ

The Resurrection

We've patched the matcha stain with denim from another rescued pair, added custom pocket embroidery, and these bad boys are ready to make memories again. Main character energy restored.
